How can we spread this petition around the country?


Since 1998, one of our clasmate's father has worked as a jeepney driver, providing unwavering support for their family. He has assisted them in various aspects, enabling her siblings to receive education in private schools. One of them has successfully become a civil engineer. Recently, when her father heard a news that our traditional jeepney would be replaced with a modern or electric version, he faced a financial challenge due to his modest income. This is why we're advocating against imposing the obligation for jeepney drivers to purchase modernized jeeps. Mandating drivers to purchase new vehicles could lead to significant debt accumulation for them. Given that many jeepney drivers operate on limited incomes, requiring them to bear the full cost of modernization might worsen their financial strain, impacting their livelihoods negatively. To ensure a smoother transition, alternative approaches, such as government subsidies, financial assistance programs, or phased implementation, could be explored to alleviate the economic burden on drivers during the modernization process.

Help amplify our petition advocating against mandating jeepney drivers to purchase modernized vehicles, which could lead to significant financial burdens. Contribute by spreading awareness through social media platforms, sharing the petition link across various online channels, engaging with local communities to discuss concerns, collaborating with transportation advocacy groups for added support, contacting local government officials to express worries, setting up physical petition signing stations in community hubs, and encouraging individuals, especially those affected, to share their personal stories about the potential economic challenges tied to the jeepney phase out. Your involvement can make a substantial impact in safeguarding the livelihoods of these drivers during the transition.
